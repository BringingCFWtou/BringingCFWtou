About BringingCFWtou

BringingCFWtou is an independent project with a clear mission: make custom firmware, jailbreaks and region hacks accessible to everyone.

No paywalls. No Discord invite gates. No paid guides. Just honest, working documentation — and now our own CFW too.

We cover a range of devices — Blu-ray players, consoles, embedded systems, and more. Every new project gets added once it's fully documented and tested.

[ PHILOSOPHY ] — When you buy a device, it belongs to you. Really to you. Software freedom isn't a luxury — it's a right. We help you exercise it.

All guides and software are used at your own risk. BringingCFWtou is not liable for any damage caused by modifications. All content is for educational purposes only.
